What is your carbon footprint?


Many of our daily activities result in greenhouse gas emissions (ex. carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide). Added together, these emissions make up YOUR carbon footprint. Each person's carbon footprint differs based on where they live, their habits, and every day choices.

What can you do to reduce your carbon footprint?


Regardless of whether you are a Carbon Bigfoot, Medium Foot, or Little Foot, there are steps you can take to reduce your carbon footprint. If we all work together, we can make a difference!

  • Use appliances and lightbulbs that are more energy efficient (ENERGY STAR®)
  • Turn down your heating thermostat in the winter and turn up your air conditioning in the summer
  • Wash your clothes in cold water and line dry them
  • Take public transportation, carpool with others, or bike when possible
  • Try to eat more organic and locally sourced foods
  • Use rechargeable batteries
  • Print double-sided!
